Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur

A Shrimad Rajchandra Mission Dharampur is a spiritual movement inspired from Shrimad Rajchandra. It was founded by Pujya Gurudevshri Rakeshji - a devotee of Shrimadji. It is headquartered in Dharampur, Gujarat and carries out social and spiritual activities across five continents..
